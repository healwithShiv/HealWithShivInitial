S.K. Shivasubramani

Psychologist, Hypnotherapist, Emotional Mastery & Excellence Coach

About

He is a certified mental health professional working with various government departments viz., law enforcement, judiciary, health & social welfare and consults at private hospitals around Chennai addressing various mental health needs among bureaucrats as well as common people. He also works with correctional centers, especially with juvenile delinquents and their rehabilitation.

He facilitates classes and conducts workshops on mindfulness, heartfulness, emotional mastery, and other related topics of modern psychology for corporates & educational institutions.

He dedicates most of his free time to research and keeps updating himself with the latest interventional methods and various psychological phenomena related to daily life.
