S.K. Shivasubramani

Psychologist, Hypnotherapist, Emotional Mastery & Excellence Coach

About

He is a certified mental health professional with extensive experience working across various government departments, including law enforcement, the judiciary, health, and social welfare. In addition to his contributions to public institutions, he also consults at private hospitals in Chennai, addressing a wide range of mental health concerns for both bureaucrats and the general public.

His work extends to correctional centers, where he specializes in rehabilitating juvenile delinquents, helping them reintegrate into society through structured mental health interventions. Passionate about mental well-being, he facilitates classes and conducts workshops on mindfulness, heartfulness, emotional mastery, and contemporary psychological practices for corporate organizations and educational institutions.
